My story

I am a multidisciplinary creator with a unique blend of technical expertise and artistic passion, constantly exploring new ways to express myself and make a meaningful impact. My journey began in a Mathematics-Informatics high school, where I specialized in intensive computer science. This foundation led me to pursue a degree in Economic Cybernetics, Statistics, and Informatics, specializing in Economic Informatics. During my studies, I delved into IT, economics, marketing, and management, gaining a good understanding of how technology and strategy intersect.


Throughout my life, I’ve dabbled in various creative fields, each shaping the person I am today. I’ve written screenplays, poetry, and texts, directed and filmed short movies, edited photos and videos, and explored modern dance, modeling, and performance equestrianism. Music has always been close to my heart, and I’ve enjoyed lessons in both piano and vocals. Acting has been a cornerstone of my development—after eight years of acting courses, I’ve honed my charisma, confidence, and ability to connect with others. These skills became invaluable during my five years as a reporter, where I not only gained media experience but also coordinated and organized events, some of which earned recognition in Argeș.


My creative journey is driven by a passion for self-expression and innovation. Whether crafting stories, leading projects, or bringing visions to life through design and performance, I thrive on challenges that push my limits. I find immense joy in creating work that resonates with others, offering fresh perspectives and inspiring change.


What I bring to the world is a fusion of creativity, technical knowledge, and a deep understanding of human connection. I aim to inspire, empower, and transform ideas into realities that leave a lasting impact. For me, every project is an opportunity to grow, to innovate, and to contribute something meaningful.
